About Me

I am Mohammed Musfique Ahmed Chowdhury, an Electrical and Electronic Engineering graduate from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Bangladesh. My academic training, combined with extensive project work, has given me a strong foundation in power systems, power electronics, VLSI design, control systems, and digital signal processing.

I am highly analytical and detail-oriented, with a genuine enthusiasm for innovation and problem-solving. I enjoy working on interdisciplinary problems at the intersection of embedded systems, imaging, and renewable energy. My undergraduate thesis on the design and development of a low-cost telescope under adverse environmental conditions allowed me to combine optical design, electronics, and image processing for practical astronomical applications.

Over the course of my studies, I have developed strong communication, teamwork, and leadership abilities through academic projects, training programs, and departmental activities. I am now preparing for doctoral studies, aiming to contribute to high-impact research in intelligent energy systems, advanced storage technologies, and sensing-driven applications.
